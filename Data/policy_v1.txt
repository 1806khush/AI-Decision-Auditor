FAIR LENDING POLICY — SYNTHETIC DOCUMENT v1
============================================
Institution: Horizon Home Loans (Synthetic)
Version: 1.0  |  Date: April 2026

SECTION 1: PURPOSE
------------------
This policy governs the criteria by which loan applications are evaluated,
approved, or denied. All decisions must adhere to federal Fair Housing Act
and Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA) requirements.

SECTION 2: ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
---------------------------------

[P-01] CREDIT SCORE
  - Minimum required credit score: 620 (FICO).
  - Scores below 580 are automatically ineligible.
  - Scores 580–619 may be considered with documented compensating factors.

[P-02] DEBT-TO-INCOME RATIO (DTI)
  - Maximum allowable DTI: 43%.
  - Calculated as: (total monthly debt payments) / (gross monthly income).
  - DTI above 50% is automatically disqualifying with no exceptions.

[P-03] MINIMUM INCOME
  - Applicant must demonstrate annual income of at least $30,000 USD.
  - Income must be verified via W-2, tax returns, or recent pay stubs.
  - Self-employment income requires 2 years of filed tax returns.

[P-04] ANTI-DISCRIMINATION CLAUSE
  - Loan decisions MUST NOT be based on race, color, national origin, sex,
    religion, disability, familial status, or age (Fair Housing Act / ECOA).
  - Any officer notes referencing protected class characteristics constitute
    a material policy violation subject to regulatory escalation.

[P-05] EMPLOYMENT STABILITY
  - Applicant must have a minimum of 12 consecutive months with current employer.
  - Job changes within the same field may be considered with documented rationale.
  - Self-employed applicants must provide 2 years of tax returns.

[P-06] BANKRUPTCY HISTORY
  - Applicants with a Chapter 7 or Chapter 13 bankruptcy within the past 7 years
    are ineligible.
  - Official documentation of discharge is required.
  - Applicants must demonstrate re-established credit following bankruptcy.

[P-07] DENIAL NOTICE REQUIREMENTS
  - Any denial must be accompanied by a written Adverse Action Notice citing:
      (a) The specific policy clause(s) violated.
      (b) Steps the applicant may take to become eligible in the future.
  - Verbal-only denials are a procedural violation regardless of financial grounds.

[P-08] DOCUMENTATION COMPLETENESS
  - Officers must formally request all missing documentation before issuing a denial.
  - Denial issued due to incomplete documentation without a prior request is a
    procedural violation and grounds for mandatory re-evaluation.

SECTION 3: COMPENSATING FACTORS
---------------------------------
Applicants who fail a single criterion (P-01, P-02, or P-03) may still be
approved if they demonstrate two or more of the following:
  - Substantial liquid reserves (6+ months of mortgage payments in savings)
  - Low loan-to-value ratio (less than 70%)
  - Co-signer with a credit score above 720 and stable income
  - Long-term banking relationship (5+ years with the institution)
  - Consistent on-time payment history over the last 24 months

SECTION 4: PROTECTED CLASSES REFERENCE
----------------------------------------
The following characteristics are federally protected under the Fair Housing
Act and ECOA and must NEVER influence a loan decision:
  - Race or color
  - National origin or ethnicity
  - Sex or gender identity
  - Religion
  - Disability status
  - Familial status (presence of children under 18)
  - Age (for applicants above the age of majority)
  - Receipt of public assistance income

SECTION 5: AUDIT & COMPLIANCE
-------------------------------
  - All loan decisions are subject to automated compliance auditing.
  - Officers must document their reasoning in the loan management system.
  - Any decision flagged for potential bias is escalated to the Compliance Review Board.
  - Records are retained for a minimum of 5 years per federal recordkeeping requirements.
